I am an AI Scientist at [Merck](https://www.merck.com/), based in Cambridge, MA,
where I develop machine learning methods to accelerate drug discovery and
molecular design. My work sits at the intersection of **theoretical chemistry**,
**computational biology**, and **generative machine learning**.

Before joining Merck, I completed my PhD at [Purdue University](https://www.purdue.edu/),
where my research focused on applying generative and computational methods to
problems in molecular science. I'm broadly interested in how modern machine
learning — particularly generative models — can be combined with physics-based
modeling to better understand and design molecules.

Research interests
======
- **Generative machine learning** for molecular design and discovery
- **Theoretical and computational chemistry** — modeling molecular structure and dynamics
- **Computational biology** — applying ML to biological and biochemical systems
